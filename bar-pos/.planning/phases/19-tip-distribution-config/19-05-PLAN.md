<objective>
Ship the operator-facing surface: an admin-only "Tip Split" Settings tab to edit the floor/bar/kitchen percentages (warn-but-allow on non-100 sums, D-01) and a separate "Tip Split" Reports panel showing the computed per-caja-close bucket distribution. Both use a label deliberately distinct from the pre-existing per-staff "Tip Distribution" tab to avoid the collision flagged in 19-RESEARCH.md Pitfall 2, while honoring D-05 (dedicated Settings tab) and D-06 (separate report panel).

Purpose: SC-1 (admin adjusts the split) and SC-3 (Settings panel) are delivered by the tab; SC-4 (close-caja report reflects the distribution) is delivered by the panel reading useTipDistributionEntry. D-07 is honored — the existing TipDistributionPanel/useStaffTips per-staff report is left entirely alone; this is a new, parallel widget.

Output: new TipDistributionSettingsTab (+ RTL test) registered in SettingsTabsPanel, new TipBucketDistributionPanel widget, and a new Reports tab wired in pages/reports.
</objective>
