<objective>
`update_role_permission` is not an RPC — it is two direct client-side table mutations: `role_permissions` INSERT/DELETE (toggle-permission feature) and `profiles.role` UPDATE (`useMutationUpdateStaffRole`). Per RESEARCH.md Open Question 3, these are low-frequency, manager+-only actions where the atomic guarantee of a wrapper RPC is disproportionate; the shipped `OfflineQueueProcessor` establishes client-side `supabase.rpc('record_audit', {...})` (Pattern 3) as a valid precedent. Wire both with a fire-and-forget `record_audit` call after the mutation succeeds.

Purpose: SC3 coverage for RBAC permission toggles and staff role changes.
Output: Two mutation hooks gain a post-success audit call; one unit test asserts both.

NOTE TO USER (Open Question 3): Pattern 3 (client-side, non-atomic) is the default recommendation for these two paths. If you want atomic wrapper RPCs instead, flag during review.
</objective>
